L2Boost

ESL §3.8 Bühlmann & Hothorn 2008 Gradient boosting view of FSε
\(r^{(m)} \leftarrow r^{(m-1)} - \varepsilon\,\langle x_{j^*}, r^{(m-1)}\rangle\, x_{j^*}, \quad j^* = \arg\max_j |\langle x_j, r\rangle|\)

Iterative Residual Fitting — Boosting View

15 0.1
L2Boost = gradient descent in function space where "weak learners" are coordinate projections. Each iteration fits the residuals by the best single predictor and adds a fraction ε of it. This is exactly forward stagewise regression viewed as functional gradient descent.

Algorithm

  1. Initialize β = 0, residuals r = y
  2. Find best predictor: j* = argmax |⟨xⱼ, r⟩|
  3. Update: βⱼ* += ε · sign(⟨xⱼ*, r⟩)
  4. Update residuals: r -= ε · ⟨xⱼ*, r⟩ · xⱼ*
  5. Repeat steps 2–4 for M iterations

Stopping at M controls regularization: small M = more shrinkage. Cross-validate M.
