Grouped Lasso

ID 167 Section 3.1 comparison / selection Group Sparsity
\( \min_\beta \left\{ \tfrac{1}{2}\|\mathbf{y} - \beta_0\mathbf{1} - \sum_\ell X_\ell\beta_\ell\|^2 + \lambda \sum_\ell \sqrt{p_\ell}\,\|\beta_\ell\|_2 \right\} \)  —  \(p_\ell\) = size of group \(\ell\), \(\|\beta_\ell\|_2\) = L2 norm within group

Book Quote

"This procedure encourages sparsity at both the group and individual levels." — ESL, Section 3.1

Guarantees

UnbiasedNo — L1/L2 regularization induces bias
Group SparseYes — entire groups shrink to zero
ConvexYes — L2 norm over groups is convex
Ind. SparseCond. — only with L1 within groups

Key Insight

The \(\sqrt{p_\ell}\) factor corrects for group size: larger groups have proportionally larger penalty, ensuring fair competition between groups of different sizes.
