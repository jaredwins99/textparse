Soft-Thresholding Operator

ID 183 Section 3.9 estimation / optimization Proximal Operator of L1
\( S(t, \lambda) = \text{sign}(t)\,(|t| - \lambda)_+ \)   —   \( = \begin{cases} t - \lambda & t > \lambda \\ 0 & |t| \leq \lambda \\ t + \lambda & t < -\lambda \end{cases} \)

Proximal Interpretation

\(S(t, \lambda) = \text{prox}_{\lambda|\cdot|}(t) = \arg\min_\beta \left\{ \tfrac{1}{2}(\beta - t)^2 + \lambda|\beta| \right\}\)

The soft-thresholding operator is the proximal operator of the L1 norm. This makes it the fundamental building block for ISTA, ADMM, and coordinate descent Lasso solvers.
