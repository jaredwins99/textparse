Guarantees

OracleYes — asymptotically identifies true support
ConvexYes — weighted L1 remains convex
Sign-consistentYes — under regularity conditions
UnbiasedNo — still biased in finite samples

Key Insight

Standard Lasso penalizes all coefficients equally. Adaptive Lasso gives small penalties to large OLS coefficients (they survive) and large penalties to small OLS coefficients (they are zeroed). This is the oracle property while maintaining a convex objective.
