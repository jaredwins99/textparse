Infinitesimal Forward Stagewise Regression (FS₀)

ESL §3.8 ε → 0 limit of FSε = Lasso path

FS₀ is the ε → 0 limit of incremental forward stagewise regression. In the continuous-time limit, FS₀ traces the same coefficient path as the lasso. The two paths are typically identical except in cases of non-uniqueness in the lasso (e.g., when two predictors are perfectly correlated).

Book Quote

"Letting ε → 0 gives the differential equation... We call this limiting procedure infinitesimal forward stagewise regression or FS₀." — ESL §3.8, p. 87

FS₀ is remarkable: a simple greedy algorithm that, in the continuous limit, produces exactly the lasso solution path. This provides an intuitive "boosting-like" interpretation of lasso.
